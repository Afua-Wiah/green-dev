Get to Know About Our Company

GreenDev Associates International Limited is an associate-driven, multi-disciplinary environmental engineering consulting and project management organisation, officially registered in Ghana under the Companies Code of 1963, Act 179.

GreenDev Associates International Limited was established to provide companies with wholesome solutions to support the pre-development phase of infrastructure projects and certification for construction and operation.

GreenDev Environmental Research Services is a subsidiary of GreenDev Associates International Limited, specialising exclusively in environmental engineering consultancy to distinguish its core environmental consultancy functions from those of its parent company, which offers a wider array of project management, sustainability, and earth-engineering consulting services.

This step has helped to assign suitable resources and schedule key specialists in order to provide direct focus for exclusively environmental consultancy engagements.
