WHO WE ARE

Get to Know About Our Company

GreenDev Environmental Research Services is an associate-based environmental and sustainability consultancy organisation incorporated in Ghana under the Companies Code of 1963, Act 179.
GreenDev Environmental Research Services was founded to focus solely on environmental engineering consultancy work and to differentiate core environmental consultancy activities from its sister company, GreenDev Associates International Limited, which provides a broader range of project management, sustainability, and earth-engineering consulting services.
This step has helped to assign suitable resources and schedule key specialists in order to provide direct focus for exclusively environmental consultancy engagements.
